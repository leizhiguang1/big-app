BIG · Build Progress

What's shipped, what's next.

BIG is a service-business management platform (starting with dental clinics, designed to also fit salons / spas / barbershops). We rebuilt it from scratch on a modern stack — Next.js 16, Supabase, TypeScript strict, shadcn/ui — with a framework-free service layer so a future backend split stays clean. This page tracks where we are.
